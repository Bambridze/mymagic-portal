PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: June 2026

1. 100% Offline Privacy Commitment

S-production built the MyMagic app as a Commercial app. This Service is provided by S-production at no cost and is intended for use as is. This application operates entirely offline and autonomously on your mobile device. We do not host any external user accounts, cloud databases, or remote tracking servers for personal information.

2. Microphone Permission (Audio Recording)

The application requires temporary access to your device's microphone via the RECORD_AUDIO permission solely for the local speech recognition engine (Vosk) to detect verbal magic commands and secret unlock phrases. All voice processing is handled inside your RAM locally. Audio streams are never recorded, never stored on the physical drive, and are never transmitted over the internet.

3. Device Storage Access

Storage read permissions are utilized exclusively via the standard Android MediaStore API to safely scan and copy custom .mp4 or .mp3 media packages placed by the user inside the local "Download" directory into the app's isolated private sandbox directory. No other internal folders are accessed or modified.

4. Children's Privacy (Age Restriction)

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. By accepting the in-app legal disclaimer, users officially confirm they are at least 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone. Since the software does not transmit data, a child's privacy is technically protected by the architecture itself.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact the High Council of S-production via our developer resources.
